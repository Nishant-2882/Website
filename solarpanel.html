Solar Panel Improviser

Components used-

  1. ACS712 Current Sensor
  2. Dust Sensor
  3. Arduino
  4. Proximity Sensor
  5. Solar Panels
  6. Capacitor
  7. Resistors
  8. Jumper Wires
  9. Breadboard

I performed an experiment using solar panel to determine the effect of dust accumulation on the solar panel on its power generation. I observed the power generated by the solar panel for two cases. For first case, I observed it by cleaning the solar panel regularly and for second case, I observed the dust accumulated on the solar panel using the dust sensor and measured the power generated by it.
