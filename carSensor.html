Smart Car Sensor

Components used-

  1. Dust Sensor
  2. Arduino
  3. Buzzer
  4. DHT11 Sensor
  5. Proximity Sensor
  6. Capacitor
  7. Resistors
  8. Jumper Wires
  9. Breadboard

I have created a car sensor that will sense the distance between the cars and give a signal through the buzzer if the distance is closer than the dangerous range. The sensor is used for other purposes, too, to determine the current weather conditions at the location by providing the value of AQI, temperature, and humidity of that location using the various sensors.
