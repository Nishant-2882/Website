Secret Morse Code Device

Components used-

  1. Arduino
  2. Buzzer
  3. Resistors
  4. Jumper Wires
  5. Breadboard
  6. LED
  7. Potentiometer
  8. Push Button

I have created a device with all the instruments provided which will be a music player to outside world but just by the click of a single button, we can convert it to a Morse code decoder used to receive messages in the form of morse code. In its music player I have programmed it to play 2 songs “ “ and “ “, and in its second form it will convert the messages received to it in the form of Morse code and display it through a LED light used in the circuit.
